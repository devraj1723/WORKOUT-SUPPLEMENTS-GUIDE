Beginner Full-Body Split

3 Days a week. Focus on compound movements and perfect form.

Goblet Squats

3 Sets x 12 Reps

The foundation of lower body strength. Focus on keeping your chest up.

Push-Ups

3 Sets x Max Reps

Building chest, shoulder, and tricep strength with just your bodyweight.

Dumbbell Rows

3 Sets x 10 Reps

Essential for back health and building a strong "pull" foundation.

Exercise Form Tips
  • Inhale on the way down, exhale on the way up.
  • Keep your core engaged throughout the movement.
  • Control the weight; don't let gravity do the work.
