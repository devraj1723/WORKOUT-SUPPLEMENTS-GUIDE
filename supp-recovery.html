Performance & Recovery

Maximize your training output and minimize downtime.

Energy

Pre-Workout

Designed to increase focus, blood flow, and energy levels during training.

Dosage: 1 Scoop 20-30 mins before training. Avoid taking late in the evening.
Endurance

BCAA (Branched-Chain Amino Acids)

Leucine, Isoleucine, and Valine help prevent muscle breakdown during long sessions.

Dosage: 5-10g sipped during your workout (Intra-workout).
Health

Fish Oil (Omega-3)

Supports joint health, heart health, and reduces inflammation.

Note: Best taken with a meal containing fats for absorption.
Metabolism

L-Carnitine

Transports fatty acids into cells to be burned for energy.

Dosage: 2g daily, preferably before exercise.
Repair

L-Glutamine

The most abundant amino acid in the body; aids in muscle tissue repair and gut health.

Dosage: 5g post-workout or before bed.
